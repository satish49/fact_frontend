Ravi was always passionate about Math through his school and college. Occasionally, he would end up guiding his colleagues, cousins, and neighbors with their math problems. His passion for mathematics kept him going when he relocated to the US and start tutoring school students for Math and other subjects. Ravi's passion lies in crafting the best version of each student. He believes students need empathy and compassion on their journey of learning. This fundamental belief guides his choices for team members and pedagogy. Ravi invests in achieving academic excellence and creating career opportunities through SAT/ACT preparation. Ravi's real motivation lies in his students' success.

As a professional, Ravi is a software architect in a consulting company. He completed his Masters in Technology from NIT Warangal, one of India's top colleges. He brings his experience-driven skills like situation and gap analysis, designing capability augmentation plans, and executing those in limited time.
